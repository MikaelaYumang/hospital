ABOUT

The main motive of eVisit HealthCare is to provide a reliable service to our users with guaranteed privacy. This website holds the details of the patient to book their appointment with the doctors and can view their previous appointments. The main objectives of this hospital management system includes: (1) display information about the doctors, nurses, and staff in the hospital; (2) feature that can schedule appointments; and (3) displaying information about hospital inventory and determine its availability

The patient can view the doctors of their specialization, so that they can book an appointment with the respective doctor of their interest.

The Receptionist in the organization will handle the outpatients coming to the hospital and maintain the payments for the consultation through offline. The Admin can add new Doctors, Receptionists, view all the appointments, view the prescriptions given to the patients by doctors.

MISSION

The mission of our hospital system can vary depending on the specific goals, values, and priorities of the healthcare organization. Here are some of it. To provide high-quality, patient-centered healthcare that focuses on the well-being, safety, and satisfaction of patients.To continuously improve the quality and safety of medical services, ensuring that patients receive the best possible care with minimal risks. To ensure equitable access to healthcare services for all members of the community. Furthermore, to support medical education and research, training the next generation of healthcare professionals.
